S4E Cricket Tournament - Highlights

‹ ›

It was the first time S4E organized Cricket Tournament. The Cricket lovers from different companies (Collabera,PMC, Bajaj Allianz, HDFC, Deepak Nitrite Limited (DNL), Indralok, Windowmaker, Suzlon, Intivia, & Other IT Companies) participated in the tournament. It was knock-out tournament and the venue was Poddar School ground, Sherkhi.

In the full day event, Collabera and DNL managed to get into the finals. After the heart throbbing knock, Collabera won the title.

Man of the Match trophies were receive by Sapan (Collabera), Rajdeep (DNL), Pratik (Windowmaker), Narendra (Suzlon), Prashant (DNL), Mayank (Collabera), and Krunal (Collabera).

Prashant (DNL) was awarded as the Best Bowler and Amit Giri (DNL) was awarded as the Best Batsman. Mayank (Collabera) got the hold on the Man of the Series trophy.

Sapan (Collabera) and Amit Giri (DNL) were awarded for hitting maximum 4s and 6s respectively.

Mayank and Ankit were awarded for the Best Catches of the tournament.
