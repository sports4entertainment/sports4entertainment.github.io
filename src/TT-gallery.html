S4E Table Tennis (Singles) Tournament - Highlights

S4E Table Tennis (Singles) Tournament was held on 27th April 2013 at TTAB, MSU Pavallion, Vadodara.

Venue Background:

The TTAB campus at MS University is renovated at level compatible with Olympics and International level Table Tennis matches. TTAB, Table Tennis Association Baroda, is affiliated to the All India Table Tennis Federation and Indian Olympics Association. TTAB campus is regularly being used for organizing district level and state level competitions.

‹ ›

The sunny morning started with the crowd of TT participants tying their shoelaces to win the title. TT Champs from different corporate such as ONGC, TCS, Allscripts, C-SAM, Talent Anywhere, ERDA, ABHS, Matrix, Investis, Rishabh, NetWeb and Windowmaker assembled together on the battle field.

Exciting matches started from the beginning as lot of excellent players clashed in their 1st round.

It was challenging to reach through the Finals as the game requires lot of stamina in addition to the talent. However Bhumit (from Allscripts), Amit (from C-Sam), Dhaval (from Investis), and Alkesh (from ONGC) made up to the semi finals. Eventually, Alkesh (ONGC) & Amit (C-Sam) qualified for the finals.

Mr. Alkesh Patel (ONGC) with the high energy and stamina ultimately won the title. He is really encouraging for the youth to build and maintain such a high level of energy throughout the tournament.

Congratulations to the winners and We would like to thank all the participants for playing with right sporting spirit.

We also heartily thank TTAB(Table Tennis Association Baroda) for their esteem support and providing the best tournament infrastructure.
